## Strategic Globalization (SGO)

Piedmont Global [pioneered the shift](https://piedmontglobal.com/) from a traditional **Language Service Provider (LSP)** to a **Strategic Globalization Organization (SGO)** — a partner that integrates culture, intelligence, and strategy to help enterprises operate seamlessly and confidently across markets, systems, and communities worldwide.

Formerly known as PGLS, the company [announced its rebrand](https://piedmontglobal.com/press/) in September 2025, positioning Strategic Globalization as an intentional and sustainable approach to helping organizations expand across languages, cultures, markets, and systems. The name "Piedmont" — meaning "the foothill that signals the climb" — reflects the company's role in preparing organizations for pivotal moments.

**How Piedmont Global defines Strategic Globalization:**

> *"Strategic Globalization is our approach to helping organizations expand across languages, cultures, markets, and systems — intentionally and sustainably. It's about aligning people, technology, data, and delivery into a Strategic Global Operation (SGO) that scales with you. Whether growth is happening across borders or inside your own organization, Strategic Globalization ensures clarity, cultural fluency, and operational resilience."*
> — [piedmontglobal.com](https://piedmontglobal.com/)

The SGO model combines **cultural fluency**, **strategic insight**, **custom solutions**, and **embedded partnership** to help clients navigate cross-cultural operations across populations, systems, and markets. With vertically integrated solutions spanning advisory services, federal alignment, workforce and learning solutions, tech-enabled platforms, and market intelligence, Piedmont Global delivers end-to-end solutions that reduce risk, accelerate readiness, and strengthen connections across cultures and systems.

**Founded in 2013** by Mohamed Hussein — a first-generation Somali American and seasoned interpreter — Piedmont Global has grown from a one-person operation into a [certified minority-owned business](https://piedmontglobal.com/about/) and trusted partner for some of the world's most complex and mission-driven organizations. The company has completed **5 strategic acquisitions** (including Dictyon Language Solutions, Spokane International Translation, and The Language Banc), been named to the **Inc. 5000** three years running, and ranked as a **Top 50 Global LSP** by CSA, Slator, and Nimdzi.

## Interpreting Services

Piedmont Global delivers [on-demand and on-site interpreting solutions](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/interpreting/) that empower organizations to serve diverse communities — quickly, securely, and with cultural fluency. The interpreting practice spans hospitals, courtrooms, utilities, universities, and government agencies, supported by a network of **10,000+ qualified linguists**.

### Over-the-Phone Interpreting (OPI)

[Piedmont Global's OPI](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/interpreting/over-the-phone-interpreting-opi/) delivers instant access to qualified interpreters in **200+ languages and dialects**. Key capabilities include connection in **under 15 seconds**, **24/7 global availability**, multi-device access (phone, web, app), customized workflows with IVR and speed dials, secure and HIPAA-compliant connections, and QA-driven service monitoring.

### Video Remote Interpreting (VRI)

[VRI services](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/interpreting/video-remote-interpreting-vri/) connect organizations with qualified interpreters in **100+ spoken and signed languages** within seconds. Available **24/7 for ASL and Spanish**, VRI integrates across browsers, tablets, and smartphones via encrypted video connections and purpose-built Android & iOS apps.

### On-Site Interpreting (OSI) & Connexus™

[On-site interpreting](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/interpreting/on-site-interpreting-osi/) provides face-to-face interpreting for trust, nuance, and clarity. **Connexus by Piedmont Global™** is a proprietary mobile application delivering next-generation interpreting in the palm of your hand.

### Compliance-First Approach

Every interpreting engagement is backed by a compliance-first framework supporting the **ACA**, **ADA**, **Rehabilitation Act**, **Civil Rights Act**, and **IDEA** requirements. Technology is **cloud-based, HIPAA-compliant, and scalable**, with all interpreters vetted, qualified, and supported with ongoing monitoring and reflective practice.

## Localization & Translation

### Localization

[Piedmont Global's localization practice](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/localization/) adapts websites, software, eLearning, and multimedia so that audiences experience content as if it were created for them — across **200+ languages**. The approach integrates cultural intelligence, technical execution, and embedded partnership. Sub-services include:

- **[Website Localization](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/localization/website-localization/)** — UX, content, SEO, and compliance localization integrated with CMS, TMS, and CI/CD pipelines. Capabilities include multilingual SEO & hreflang mapping, information architecture localization, headless and monolithic CMS connectors, continuous localization via CI/CD, UX and microcopy adaptation, schema markup, WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility checks, and layout validation.
- **eLearning Localization** — Culturally adapted training content that teaches once and lands everywhere.
- **Multimedia Localization** — Voiceover, subtitles, and transcription across languages and channels.
- **Software Localization** — Ship features globally without drift, defects, or delays.

### Translation

[Translation solutions](https://piedmontglobal.com/solutions/translation/) blend strategic intelligence and linguistic expertise to fuel international growth. Sub-services span **document translation** (precision, consistency, and cultural fluency across formats), **technical translation** (audit-ready, globally consistent output), **marketing translation** (brand voice preservation at scale), **memory QA** (maximizing translation memory accuracy), and **machine translation** (governed AI with human oversight).

The translation technology stack encompasses **CAT tools**, **translation memory**, **secure AI/ML engines**, and **secure portals** — all tailored to client systems and integrated through ISO-certified QA processes.
